'''Projector''' ('''Zachary Williams''') is a fictional character, a [[Mutant (Marvel Comics)|mutant]] villain from [[Marvel Comics]].

==Fictional character biography==
Projector is one of the newer members of the [[Acolytes (comics)|Acolytes]], and first appeared in [[Quicksilver (comics)|Quicksilver]] #9. He was later seen on [[Genosha]].{{issue|date=January 2013}}

Projector has not been seen for some time after [[Cassandra Nova]]'s [[Sentinel (comics)|Sentinels]] destroyed Genosha, killing 16 million mutants, but he did survive the attack.

===After M-Day/Messiah Complex===
Projector is one of the few that survived the assault on Genosha, and retained his mutant powers during the [[Decimation (comics)|M-Day]].{{issue|date=January 2013}} He later made his return in the event, ''[[X-Men: Messiah Complex]]'' as a member of a group of [[Acolytes (comics)#Ex-Acolytes|former Acolytes]] who were hiding out on their own.<ref name=UXMEN492>''Uncanny X-Men'' #492</ref> They were tracked down by the X-Men looking for information on the whereabout of Exodus and his current group of Acolytes. Projector and his teammates fought back, but they were outmatched, and after taking a vicious beating from Wolverine, Projector cracked and told them what he knew.<ref name=UXMEN492/>

==Powers and abilities==
Projector could emanate an unknown energy force allowing him to form solid energy constructs and forcefields around himself or others, shaping them into simple forms like ramps, spheres, and pillars. These fields appear to behave as physical constructs and are not indestructible.<ref name=UXMEN492/>
