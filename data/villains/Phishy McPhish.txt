'''Phishy McPhish''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[Mutant (Marvel Comics)|mutant]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe. His first appearance was in ''The Brotherhood #7''.

==Fictional character biography==
This unnamed mutant gave [[X (Marvel Comics)|Hoffman]] a pep talk before the press conference in [[Philadelphia]].{{issue|date=January 2013}} His powers, if any, were unrevealed.

==Note==
His name is never given in the series; "Phishy McPhish" is a name given to him by Marvel handbook author Jeff Christansen and his staff at the [http://www.marvunapp.com/Appendix/brothrhx.htm Appendix to the Handbook of the Marvel Universe], who claim that he "listens to too much [[Phish]]".
