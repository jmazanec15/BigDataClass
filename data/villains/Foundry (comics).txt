'''Foundry''' is a fictional character appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]].

==Fictional character biography==
His first appearance was in ''Force Works #6'' as a member of the [[Mandarin's Avatars]]. In the first issue of "Heroes For Hire" Vol2, Foundry is defeated by [[Misty Knight]].

==Notes==
*A member of the [[Blood (comics)|Blood]] is called '''Foundry'''.  It first appeared in ''Midnight Sons Unlimited'' #4
