'''Vertigo''' is a fictional character appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]]. She first appeared in ''Fantastic Four'' #186.

==Fictional character biography==
Vertigo was a member of the group of witches known as [[Salem's Seven]] led by [[Nicholas Scratch]].{{issue|date=January 2011}} She is the daughter of Scratch, and granddaughter of [[Agatha Harkness]].

[[Wizard (Marvel Comics)|Wizard]] reformed the [[Frightful Four]] using [[Gazelle (Marvel Comics)|Gazelle]], [[Reptilla]], and Vertigo of [[Salem's Seven]], and they attacked Chicago to get the attention of [[Mister Fantastic]]. Mister Fantastic was almost defeated by the Frightful Four until [[Scarlet Witch]] appeared to help him.<ref>''[[Fantastic Four]]'' Vol. 5 #10</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
Vertigo was able to induce sensations of extreme vertigo in her opponents.
