'''Tether''' is a fictional [[Mutant (Marvel Comics)|mutant]] character appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]]. She first appeared in ''Uncanny X-Men Annual 1997'' and was created by Jorge Gonzalez, [[Duncan Rouleau]], and Troy Hubbs.

==Fictional character biography==
Tether was a member of [[Gene Nation]], the group of second generation [[Morlocks (comics)|Morlocks]] whom [[Storm (Marvel Comics)|Storm]] encountered and relocated to [[Africa]]. When [[Humanity's Last Stand]] attacked the Gene Nationals, Storm and the [[X-Men]] arrived to help. Unfortunately, so did [[Havok (comics)|Havok]]'s [[Brotherhood of Mutants]] who offered the Gene Nationals protection against Humanity's Last Stand. Humanity's Last Stand were eventually defeated, and Tether (along with fellow Gene National [[Boost (comics)|Boost]]) sought to leave, helping the Brotherhood escape in return. Though they don't have plans to join up with them.<ref>''Uncanny X-Men Annual 1997''</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
Tether is a mutant with a reptilian appearance. She possesses razor-sharp claws and fangs and an electrified tail.
