'''Andrew Forson''' (also known as the '''[[Scientist Supreme]]''') is a fictional character appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]].

==Publication history==
{{expand-section|date=March 2016}}
Andrew Forson first appeared in ''[[Fantastic Four (comic book)|Fantastic Four]]'' #610 and was created by [[Jonathan Hickman]] and [[Ryan Stegman]]. 

==Fictional character biography==
Forson was the Scientist Supreme of [[Advanced Idea Mechanics|A.I.M.]] after the organization became public and established their base in the now sovereign island Barbuda and overthrew the [[Wizard (Marvel Comics)|Wizard]] with consent of the rest of A.I.M. Barbuba was then renamed A.I.M. Island.<ref>''Fantastic Four'' #610 (November 2012)</ref>

Forson appears as the Supreme Leader of the new High Council of A.I.M. consisting of [[Graviton (comics)|Graviton]] (as the Minister of Science), [[Jude the Entropic Man]] (as the [[Health minister|Minister of Health]]), [[Mentallo]] (as the Minister of Home Affairs), [[Superia]] (as the [[Education minister|Minister of Education]] in Bagalia), the female [[Black Widow (Yelena Belova)|Super-Adaptoid]] (as the [[Minister of State]] in Bagalia), and the undercover [[Taskmaster (comics)|Taskmaster]] (as the [[Defence minister|Minister of Defense]]).<ref>''Secret Avengers'' Vol. 2 #2 (March 2013)</ref> Forson then leads A.I.M. into attending a weapons expo which led to A.I.M. fighting against the [[Secret Avengers]]. During the battle, Forson takes the opportunity to steal the [[Iron Patriot]] armor.<ref>''Secret Avengers'' Vol. 2 #3 (April 2013)</ref> [[Daisy Johnson]] launched an unsanctioned operation to send the Secret Avengers to A.I.M. Island to assassinate Forson, and the group seemingly killed him. Despite Johnson ended up suspended for breaking protocol and [[Maria Hill]] is put in charge of S.H.I.E.L.D. again, Forson was revealed to be alive all along, and the news of A.I.M. being a new permanent member of the Security Council is known.<ref>''Secret Avengers'' Vol. 2 #5 (June 2013)</ref> 

Forson finds Superia when she is badly injured after an incident on A.I.M. Island regarding an escaped creature.<ref>''Avengers'' Vol. 5 #15</ref> An A.I.M. Agent then approaches Forson and Superia showing them a 2-D hologram of the Organism beating the Avengers. Taking pride in this, Forson orders their translocator activated and to retrieve their "lost child".<ref>''Avengers'' Vol. 5 #16 (July 2013)</ref>

When Taskmaster is shot and seemingly killed by Mockingbird now under Forson's control,<ref>''Secret Avengers'' Vol. 2 #13 (February 2014)</ref> Forson revealed that Mockingbird was secretly working for Forson years ago, and that he used Mockingbird to "get [[Victorius (comics)|Victorious]] out of the way" so that he could take over the Cult of Entropy.<ref>''Secret Avengers'' Vol. 2 #14 (March 2014)</ref> Forson also uses [[Super-Adaptoid|Adaptoids]] from an alternate reality to combat the Avengers.<ref>''Avengers'' Vol. 5 #25-28 (January-April 2014)</ref>

Using an as-yet-unidentified device, Forson and A.I.M. accelerate the flow of time in the limits of A.I.M. Island creating in a matter of hours for the real world year of progress and transforming A.I.M. into a technologically advanced empire.<ref>''Avengers World'' #1</ref> When [[Smasher]] comes to the island, Forson has the Entropic Man transform her into his messenger.<ref>''Avengers World'' #2 (January 2014)</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
Andrew Forson is an experienced scientist with genius-level intellect.
